frequently asked questions

FAQS

What is your check in/ check out times?

Check-In hours are from 1-6pm. If you arrive after 7 pm, we do charge a late check-in fee of R200. Check-Out time is at 10:00 am.

Do you allow Pets?

We apologize but the Lodge does not allow outside pets.

What payment method do you accept?

We accept cash, EFT (for deposit only), and credit cards (Visa/Mastercard) as payment methods. A 50% deposit is required to reserve a room and the balance is due upon check-in.

Is smoking permitted at the lodge?

Citi Lodge is smoke free indoors. Smoking is permitted outdoors. R500 will be charged for any policy violations.

Do the bedrooms have refrigerators?

Yes, the lodge has refrigeretors in every unit.
